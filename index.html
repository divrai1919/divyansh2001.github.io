ABOUT DISTRICT

Varanasi (Vārāṇasī; Hindustani pronunciation: [ʋaːˈraːɳəsi] (About this soundlisten)),
also known as Kashi (Kāśī; [kaːʃiː]), Benares[11] or Banaras (Banāras; [bəˈnaːrəs] (About this soundlisten)),
is a city on the banks of the river Ganges in Uttar Pradesh, India,
320 kilometres (200 mi) south-east of the state capital, Lucknow, and 121 kilometres (75 mi) east of Allahabad.
